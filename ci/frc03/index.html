Scouting Application
FRC 3

Corequisites: CS 6 or CS 9

Introduction

The following is an overview of how to scout an FRC match.

Instructions

At some point, you will be tasked with a single robot to review during their match. You must watch this robot at all times.

Note how well it synergizes with other types of robots.

On the scouting sheet provided there should be columns for different aspects of play. Give points, checks in columns, or checks in boxes as appropriate.

Give the scouting sheet to the person tabulating the results upon completion of the match.
