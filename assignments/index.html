Turning In Assignments

When a topic's problems have been completed and tested to one's satisfaction, do the following:

  1. Compress the source files into a .zip or .tar.gz archive
  2. Email the archive to software@spartatroniks.com as an attachment
    • The Subject line of the email should be "<first name> <last initial> - Topic #"
    • For example, if John Doe is submitting source for topic 2, then the subject line would be "John D - Topic 2"

Once we have verified that the programs are valid solutions to the problems, we will update the table above.
