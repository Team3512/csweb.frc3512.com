Control Theory 102
Motion Profiling

Prerequisite: CT 101

Introduction

When the response of PID controllers we tune needs to be more controlled, we change the setpoint over time in a controlled manner using a motion profile. This article talks about different types of motion profiles and what each of them provide. Read from the beginning until the section “Making Your Point-to-Point”.

For most of our control applications, we use trapezoidal motion profiles. FRC teams 254 and 971 gave a talk at FIRST World Championships in 2015 about motion profiles.
